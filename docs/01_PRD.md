# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Target Audience
**Dental Practice Owners, Private Medical Clinic Operators, and NHS GP Practice Partners.** 
These key decision-makers care about practice efficiency, reducing clinical workload, and maximizing income (whether that means capturing lost private revenue or hitting NHS QOF targets). They do not want more admin work.

## The Problem
**Patient Non-Adherence (Behavioral Problem):** Patients fail to attend scheduled appointments (DNAs) and fail to attend essential chronic care follow-ups (e.g., annual diabetic reviews). 
**The Practice Impact (Business Problem):**
1. Missed appointments waste valuable clinical time and worsen the "8am gridlock" queue.
2. Missed chronic care reviews directly result in lost QOF bonus points, heavily reducing partner income.

## The Solution
An elegant, fast, interactive **Decision Intelligence web tool**. 
The practice manager types in their practice name or postcode. Using public NHS data, the tool does three things:
1. Calculates exactly how much clinical time and QOF revenue they lost last month due to non-adherence.
2. Compares them against their exact "Peers" (practices of similar size and demographics in similar areas), rather than a useless national average.
3. Warns them against flawed operational fixes (working longer hours) and offers specific, proven behavioral interventions to **re-write how they are already communicating** (e.g., changing generic SMS reminders to specific, cost-aware nudges).

## Unique Value Proposition
Unlike clinical system dashboards (which only show raw numbers), this tool uses:
1. **Data Science (Clustering):** It doesn't compare a rural NHS practice to a high-end London dental clinic. It automatically groups a user only with practices that look exactly like theirs (similar size, local demographics) so the benchmark is undeniable.
2. **Behavioral Science (The EASY Framework):** It visually shows them *why* they are failing by identifying the "Root Cause" (the high-risk cohort). It then offers behavioral "nudges" based on the **EASY framework (Easy, Attractive, Social, Timely)** to optimize their existing systems. It doesn't ask them to buy new software; it asks them to rewrite their current SMS templates and re-target their reception calls.
3. **Instant ROI Calculation:** It translates annoyance into a quantifiable financial problem with an undeniable fix. (e.g., "If you are losing £4,000 this month, a £1,000 investment in targeted, EASY-framework SMS messaging for the 'guilt' cohort will reduce that loss by 50%").
